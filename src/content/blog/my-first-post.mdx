## Why Write?

Mathematics is often seen as a solitary pursuit, but I believe that sharing ideas is one of the best ways to deepen understanding. As the great Paul Halmos once said:

> "The only way to learn mathematics is to do mathematics."

Writing about mathematics forces me to think clearly, organize my thoughts, and identify gaps in my understanding. I hope that by sharing my notes and insights, I can help others on their mathematical journey as well.
